Modern Living in Tokyo's Creative Hub

Welcome to Akima Shimokitazawa, your perfect home away from home in one of Tokyo's most vibrant neighborhoods. Our apartments offer a unique blend of comfort, style, and authentic Japanese living experience.

Located in a quiet high-end residential area while being just minutes away from the bustling center of Shimokitazawa, our spaces are designed for creative professionals and digital nomads looking for an extended stay in Tokyo.

Fully equipped with everything you need for comfortable long-term living, our apartments allow you to immerse yourself in Tokyo's culture while maintaining the productivity and comfort you need.
